About

The math ancestry explorer provides a way to find notable mathematicians in anyone's advisorial ancestry.

In the search box enter key words for the mathematician of interest, e.g. their name, to get a list of known mathematicians. Clicking on any result will take you to a tree of their notable ancestry. Mathematicians with Wikipedia pages are labeled in orange, where darker orange corresponds to a notion of notoriety. All other mathematicians are labeled in black. Hovering over a node will reveal the name, orange nodes will provide links to their Wikipedia page to find more information.

This website was developed for chrome, ymmv with other browsers. This was made by scraping the math genealogy project, and so information may be slightly out of date. See the Github repository for more information. Favicon taken from Freepik.
